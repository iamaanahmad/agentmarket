# SecurityGuard AI Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024  
**Version:** 1.0.0

## 1. Introduction

SecurityGuard AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our blockchain security analysis service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Wallet Address**: Your Solana wallet address for authentication and payment processing
- **Transaction Data**: Blockchain transaction data you submit for security analysis (processed temporarily)
- **Username**: Optional username for account identification
- **Communication Data**: Messages sent through our security chat interface

### 2.2 Information We Collect Automatically
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use our service, including scan frequency and patterns
- **Technical Data**: IP address, browser type, device information, and access times
- **Performance Data**: Response times, error rates, and service performance metrics

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- **Private Keys**: We never request or store your private keys or seed phrases
- **Personal Identity**: We do not collect names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses
- **Financial Details**: We do not store credit card information or traditional banking details

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Purposes
- **Security Analysis**: Analyzing blockchain transactions for security threats and vulnerabilities
- **Service Provision**: Providing real-time transaction scanning and security recommendations
- **Authentication**: Verifying your identity through wallet signature verification
- **Payment Processing**: Processing micropayments for service usage through blockchain escrow

### 3.2 Secondary Purposes
- **Service Improvement**: Analyzing usage patterns to improve our security detection algorithms
- **Security Monitoring**: Detecting and preventing abuse, fraud, and security threats
- **Compliance**: Meeting legal and regulatory requirements
- **Research**: Improving blockchain security through anonymized threat intelligence

## 4. Data Retention and Storage

### 4.1 Session-Only Data Processing
- **Transaction Data**: Processed only during your active session and immediately deleted
- **Scan Results**: Stored temporarily in encrypted cache for performance optimization (5-minute TTL)
- **Chat History**: Maintained only during your active session for context

### 4.2 Persistent Data (Minimal)
- **Wallet Address**: Stored for authentication and payment tracking
- **Usage Statistics**: Anonymized metrics for service improvement
- **Security Logs**: Audit trails for security and compliance (90-day retention)

### 4.3 Data Location
- **Primary Storage**: Encrypted databases in secure cloud infrastructure
- **Cache Storage**: Encrypted Redis cache with automatic expiration
- **Backup Storage**: Encrypted backups with geographic distribution

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 We Do NOT Share Your Data With
- Third-party advertisers or marketing companies
- Data brokers or analytics companies
- Social media platforms
- Government agencies (except as legally required)

### 5.2 Limited Sharing Scenarios
- **Service Providers**: Encrypted data with essential infrastructure providers (AWS, Redis)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law, court order, or regulatory compliance
- **Security Threats**: Anonymized threat intelligence sharing with blockchain security community
- **Business Transfer**: In the event of merger, acquisition, or asset sale (with user notification)
